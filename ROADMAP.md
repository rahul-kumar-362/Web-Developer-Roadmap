## 🎯 **Overview**

This roadmap is designed to take you from **complete beginner** to **professional web developer** in **28 weeks** (7 months). Each phase builds upon the previous one, ensuring you have a solid foundation before moving forward.

**Time Commitment**: 15-20 hours per week
**Total Duration**: 28 weeks (7 months)

---

## 📊 **Learning Timeline**

```
Week 1-4:   Foundation (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git)
Week 5-8:   Frontend (Advanced JS, Responsive Design)
Week 9-12:  React.js (Components, Hooks, State Management)
Week 13-16: Backend (Node.js, Express, APIs)
Week 17-20: Databases (SQL, MongoDB, Design)
Week 21-24: Advanced (Next.js, TypeScript, Testing)
Week 25-28: Projects (Portfolio, Full-Stack Apps)
```
